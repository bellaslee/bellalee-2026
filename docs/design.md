# Core aesthetic

Think:

- soft porcelain white
- blue-gray ink
- restrained detail
- lots of breathing room
- subtle ornamental influence, not literal grandma china wallpaper

The vibe should feel quiet, refined, editorial, with a little handcrafted
character.

# Color palette

Use a very tight palette.

Base

- Porcelain: #F8F7F3
- Warm ivory: #F2EFE8

Primary ink

- Sapphire blue: #1C2985
- Deep indigo ink: #213548

Secondary

- Mist blue wash: rgba(208, 215, 244, 0.38)
- Dusty periwinkle: #707AB3

Accent

- Soft gold-gray: #B9B1A3
- Border tint: rgba(28, 41, 133, 0.18)

How to use it:

- Background: porcelain / warm ivory
- Headings and body copy: deep indigo
- Buttons, links, chips, and emphasis: sapphire blue
- Cards and subtle fills: mist blue wash at low opacity
- Accent linework: soft gold-gray very sparingly
- Borders and selection states: transparent sapphire tint

Do not overdo the blue. The porcelain effect comes more from restraint +
contrast + spacing than from making everything blue.

# Fonts

- Headings: Cormorant Garamond
- Body/UI: Inter

## Type scale:

- H1: 64–80 px
- H2: 36–44 px
- H3: 24–28 px
- Body: 16–18 px
- Small/meta: 13–14 px
